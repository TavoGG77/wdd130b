Temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Quetzaltenango

About the temple

The Quetzaltenango Guatemala Temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, dedicated in 2011, serves members in Western Guatemala, offering sacred ordinances in a Mayan-inspired setting with local volcanic landscape art, featuring a single spire with an angel Moroni statue and beautiful grounds open for public reflection, symbolizing peace and Christ's teachings

Dedicated Dec 11, 2011: Dedicated Prayer
